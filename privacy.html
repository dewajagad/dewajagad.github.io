Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What Information We Collect

SmartStockUS collects minimal personal information. If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address and the date of subscription. We do not collect names, addresses, payment information, or any other sensitive personal data.

Our website may use standard analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) that collect anonymized data about site traffic, including pages visited, time on site, geographic region (country/city level), and device type. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

How We Use Your Information

Email addresses collected through newsletter signup are used exclusively to send the SmartStockUS weekly newsletter. We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

This site may use cookies for analytics purposes (see above). You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect your experience on some pages.

Advertising

SmartStockUS may display advertisements, including those served by Google AdSense or similar platforms. These ad networks may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your ad network settings or by visiting the Google Ad Settings page.

Third-Party Links

This website contains links to third-party websites, including brokerages, tools, and other resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

Your Rights

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email. To request deletion of your email from our list, contact us at our contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
